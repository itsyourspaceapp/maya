Request Account Deletion - Maya

If you would like to delete your account and associated data from the Maya app, please either use the 'Delete Account' option in the app or send a request to:

Email: itsyourspaceapp@gmail.com

What will be deleted?

  • Your account
  • All associated personal information

What will be retained?

No data will be retained after your account is deleted.

How long will deletion take?

All deletion requests are processed immediately via the app or within 7 business days when requested via mail.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us via the email above.
